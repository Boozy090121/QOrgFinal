Implementation Timeline

Track the reorganization implementation timeline with phases, activities, and milestones.

Phase 1: Planning & Analysis

January 2025 - February 2025

Initial planning and analysis of the current quality organization structure. Identify gaps and opportunities for improvement.

Activities

  • Current State Assessment

    Analyze current quality organization structure and identify pain points.

    Assignee: Quality Director

  • Stakeholder Interviews

    Conduct interviews with key stakeholders to gather requirements and expectations.

    Assignee: Quality Managers

  • Gap Analysis

    Identify gaps between current state and desired future state.

    Assignee: Quality Systems Lead

Phase 2: Design & Development

March 2025 - April 2025

Design the new quality organization structure for each focus factory. Define roles, responsibilities, and reporting relationships.

Activities

  • Role Definition

    Define roles and responsibilities for each position in the new organization structure.

    Assignee: Quality Director & Managers

  • Organizational Chart Development

    Create organizational charts for each focus factory.

    Assignee: Quality Systems Lead

  • Budget Planning

    Develop budget plans for the new organization structure.

    Assignee: Finance & Quality Director
